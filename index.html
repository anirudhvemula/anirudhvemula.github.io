About

I bring a unique mix of deep technical knowledge, practical execution, and people-focused leadership, with over 15 years of experience across civil engineering, construction management, and global project operations.


I'm a licensed Professional Engineer (California) with degrees from UIUC and IIT Roorkee, and I've worked across diverse industries, from US infrastructure and consulting to managing a 60+ person Architecture team.


My path hasn’t been linear and that’s my strength. I’ve navigated global transitions, career pivots, leadership challenges, and even personal setbacks with a relentless focus on learning, delivery, and integrity. I don’t chase titles— I build relationships, trust, structure, and solutions.


What I bring:

  • End-to-end project leadership across US & India delivery models
  • Talent development, operational mentoring, and client coordination
  • Systems thinking with an eye for scalable, sustainable growth
  • A calm, grounded presence in high-pressure or ambiguous situations

I'm always open to collaborating on meaningful work so please reach out if you're seeking someone who combines technical clarity, operational depth, and steady leadership.
